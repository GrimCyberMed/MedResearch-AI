## Research Guidelines for Systematic Reviews

### Institutional Requirements

**Ethics Approval**:
- Required for systematic reviews involving human participants data
- Submit to EUC Research Ethics Committee
- Approval timeline: 4-6 weeks
- Contact: research.ethics@euc.ac.cy

**Research Registration**:
- All systematic reviews must be registered with institutional research office
- PROSPERO registration required before data extraction
- Internal registration form: Available from Graduate School

**Data Management**:
- Follow EUC Research Data Management Policy
- Store data on institutional servers or approved cloud services
- Retention period: Minimum 5 years post-publication
- Open data sharing encouraged (where ethical)

### Funding and Support

**Internal Funding**:
- **Research Seed Funding**: Up to €5,000 for pilot systematic reviews
- **Graduate Research Grants**: €2,000-€10,000 for PhD students
- **Publication Support**: Up to €2,000 for open access fees

**Application Process**:
- Submit proposal to Research Office
- Deadline: Twice yearly (March 1, September 1)
- Decision timeline: 6-8 weeks

**External Funding Sources**:
- Cyprus Research and Innovation Foundation (RIF)
- European Union Horizon Europe
- Marie Skłodowska-Curie Actions (MSCA)

### Library Resources

**Database Access** (via EUC Library):
- ✅ PubMed/MEDLINE (free, no institutional access needed)
- ✅ Cochrane Library (institutional subscription)
- ✅ PsycINFO (institutional subscription)
- ✅ CINAHL (institutional subscription)
- ✅ Scopus (institutional subscription)
- ✅ Web of Science (institutional subscription)

- Available for articles not in EUC collection

**Reference Management**:

**Research Support Services**:
- Systematic review workshops (quarterly)

## Systematic Review Workflow at EUC

### Stage 1: Planning (Weeks 1-4)

**Actions**:
1. Develop research question with supervisor/collaborators
2. Conduct scoping search to assess feasibility
3. Register with EUC Research Office
4. Apply for ethics approval (if needed)
5. Apply for internal funding (if needed)

**Deliverables**:
- Research question (PICO/SPIDER)
- Scoping search results
- Ethics application (if applicable)
- Funding application (if applicable)

### Stage 2: Protocol Development (Weeks 5-8)

**Actions**:
1. Develop full protocol using EUC template
2. Consult with research librarian for search strategy
3. Register protocol on PROSPERO
4. Submit protocol to supervisor/committee for approval

**Deliverables**:
- Complete protocol document
- PROSPERO registration number
- Approved search strategies
- Data extraction form

**EUC Resources**:
- Protocol template available from Research Office
- Librarian consultation for search strategy
- Statistical consultation for analysis plan

### Stage 3: Searching (Weeks 9-12)

**Actions**:
1. Execute searches in all planned databases
2. Export citations to reference manager
3. Deduplicate citations
4. Document search process

**Deliverables**:
- Citation library (deduplicated)
- Search documentation
- PRISMA flow diagram (identification stage)

**EUC Resources**:
- Database access via library
- Zotero institutional account
- Interlibrary loan for full texts

### Stage 4: Screening and Selection (Weeks 13-20)

**Actions**:
1. Title/abstract screening (dual independent)
2. Full-text review (dual independent)
3. Resolve conflicts
4. Document exclusions

**Deliverables**:
- Included studies list
- Excluded studies log with reasons
- PRISMA flow diagram (complete)

**EUC Resources**:
- Rayyan access (free web-based tool)
- Research assistant support (if funded)

### Stage 5: Data Extraction (Weeks 21-32)

**Actions**:
1. Pilot extraction form
2. Dual independent extraction
3. Risk of bias assessment
4. Contact authors for missing data

**Deliverables**:
- Extraction database
- Risk of bias assessments
- Author contact log

**EUC Resources**:
- REDCap access for data extraction
- Statistical consultation

### Stage 6: Analysis and Synthesis (Weeks 33-40)

**Actions**:
1. Descriptive synthesis
2. Meta-analysis (if appropriate)
3. Subgroup and sensitivity analyses
4. GRADE assessment

**Deliverables**:
- Analysis results
- Forest plots
- GRADE evidence profile

**EUC Resources**:
- R/RStudio access
- Statistical consultation
- Meta-analysis workshop materials

### Stage 7: Writing and Dissemination (Weeks 41-52)

**Actions**:
1. Write manuscript (PRISMA-compliant)
2. Internal peer review
3. Submit to journal
4. Deposit in institutional repository

**Deliverables**:
- Complete manuscript
- Supplementary materials
- PRISMA checklist

**EUC Resources**:
- English editing service
- Publication support
- Open access funding

**Total Timeline**: ~12 months (typical for PhD systematic review)
